## Relevance to Usman's partial fix

Usman's PR (https://github.com/tetherto/wdk-data-shard-wrk/pull/186) partially fixed the failing `syncBalances` job which is the backend job responsible for populating balance trend data.

Key points from the March 5 meeting/analysis (_tasks/2-march-2026/5-march-26-fix-Usman-ticket/task.txt):
- The syncBalances job makes excessive RPC calls and takes >1 hour under load
- Usman's fix addressed one subset of failures but the root cause (performance/scalability) remains
- The balance trends endpoint was returning empty entries under stress-test conditions, tied to the same balances job problems
- Production appeared stable at the time, but the issue was expected to resurface at scale

The current ticket confirms it has resurfaced: the trend endpoint is returning empty data on production for real users.
