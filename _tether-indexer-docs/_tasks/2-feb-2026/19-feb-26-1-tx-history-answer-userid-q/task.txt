In this response, we have fields fromUserId and toUserId . Both of these fields are difficult to populate given our backend architecture. The reason: The rumble-data-shard-wrk/wdk-data-shard-wrk separates users into separate shards. As an example, sender ( fromUserId ) could belong to shard-1 and receivr (toUserId ) could belong to shard-2 and there is no way for us to actually figure out the user from an address on a different shard, at the wdk-data-shard-wrk  layer (Note: we can do this only at app-node or ork level).
This limitation results in two options A) we don't provide these fields or B) we go completely against our backend architecture and fetch this information from the ork layer.
What do you guys recommend? CC: @Vigan @Mo Elsabry @Francesco C.
