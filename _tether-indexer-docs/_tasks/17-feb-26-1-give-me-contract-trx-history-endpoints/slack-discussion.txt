Hey @Vigan
looking to implement your suggested responses, I think we are dropping quite a bit of what rather can be needed. These are:

1- Dropped underlyingTransfers[]
Without this the app loses the ability to show individual transfer details (e.g., BTC: sent 0.5 to recipient + 0.3 change back). It's acceptable if the app only shows list views. But for detail views, it can fall back to the old /token-transfers endpoint, which is what we are trying to avoid.

2- Dropped Rumble appTip.appContent.message
Without this, rant messages (the text the user typed) won't be in the response. If the app needs to display Great stream! on a rant entry, it has no way to get it from this endpoint.

3- Dropped Rumble appTip.counterparty (displayName, avatarUrl)
If this is replaced by fromUserId/toUserId, the app must make separate calls to resolve usernames/avatars. This adds app complexity and latency (N+1 resolution or batch lookup).

4- Dropped appContext.referenceId
With this, if the FE ever needs to link back to the webhook/internal event, it can't. The Rumble side is under-specified.

What do you think?
